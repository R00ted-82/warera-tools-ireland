Profit by product

Net / PP = profit per production point at the best region bonus. Max / day = Net/PP × your daily throughput (if 100% of it went to that one product). Actual / day = what your companies currently make of it.

How this works · methodology
  • Net / PP = (salePrice − Σ(recipe × rawPrice)) × (1 + bonus) ÷ productionPoints. Recipes & production points from gameConfig; prices from the WarEra market (warerastats); the production bonus from the same region/country model the Migration Advisor uses (best region shown).
  • Daily throughput ≈ your own + your employees' output (production × energy × 0.343 PP/day each) plus each company's automated-engine production. It's an estimate — edit the field to match your real numbers.
  • Max / day assumes 100% of that throughput goes to one product. Actual / day sums what your companies currently produce of each item at their own region's bonus.
  • Items not on the warerastats feed (e.g. wood, paper) are priced from the live market order book (tradingOrder.getTopOrders — midpoint of best bid/ask).
  • Irish-citizens only (?bypass=1 lifts it).
