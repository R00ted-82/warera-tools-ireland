What this does

Add any Irish War Era player to the watch list and this tool snapshots their wealth every few hours, then charts how it moves over time — total, or broken down into companies, items, money, equipment and weapons (the same figures shown on the in-game profile). Toggle the timeline by day, week or month.

How this works
  • The wealth breakdown is public: user.getUserById returns stats.wealth = { companies, items, money, equipments, weapons, total } — the exact figures on the profile.
  • A GitHub Action (wealth_log.py) runs every 6 hours, snapshots each monitored player and appends to data/wealth-history.json. The chart buckets those snapshots by day, week or month in your browser.
  • Adding or removing a player posts to the same warera-proxy Worker the other tools use, which fires a repository_dispatch; a second Action edits monitored-users.json. That's the ~1 minute lag.
  • “Total” is stats.wealth.total (the exact sum of the five components), which can differ slightly from the cached userWealth ranking figure.
  • Only Irish citizens can be added (checked both in the browser and again server-side). Append ?bypass=1 to the URL to lift the browser-side gate for admin or debugging.
