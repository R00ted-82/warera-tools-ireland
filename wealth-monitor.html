How this works
  • The wealth breakdown is public: user.getUserById returns stats.wealth = { companies, items, money, equipments, weapons, total } — the exact figures on the profile.
  • A GitHub Action snapshots every monitored player every 6 hours and appends to data/wealth-history.json. The chart buckets those snapshots by day, week or month in your browser. Periods with no snapshots (e.g. before a player was added, or while they were removed) show as real gaps in the line.
  • Adding or removing a player posts to the warera-proxy Worker, which fires a repository_dispatch; a second Action edits monitored-users.json. That's the ~1 minute lag.
  • Only Irish citizens can be monitored (checked in the browser and again server-side). Append ?bypass=1 to the URL to lift the browser-side gate for admin or debugging.
